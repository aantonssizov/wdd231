Our Coaches!

Mike Stenly

A former competitive paddler turned coach, Mike brings over 15 years of experience navigating Germany's rivers and lakes. Known for his calm demeanor and laser-sharp technique, he specializes in training intermediate and advanced athletes for endurance races. Mike's coaching style blends precision drills with mental resilience training, and he's often spotted at the Main River before sunrise, charting new routes for his team.

Thomas Fray

Thomas is the strategist of the trio, with a reputation for turning underdogs into champions. When not coaching, Thomas contributes to canoeing journals and speaks at regional sports conferences.
