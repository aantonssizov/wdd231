Membership Levels

NP Membership

NP Membership

The NP (Non-Paid) membership is free and provides access to community updates, newsletters, and networking opportunities. Members at this level can attend standard events at full price and enjoy limited previews of training resources. It's a great way to stay connected without financial commitment.

Bronze Membership

Bronze Membership

The Bronze membership is the entry-level paid tier, offering all NP benefits plus added value. Bronze members receive discounts on select events and workshops, access to members-only training sessions, and recognition in the community directory. They also enjoy priority registration for popular events, giving them a head start on opportunities.

Silver Membership

Silver Membership

The Silver membership builds on Bronze with even greater perks. Members receive larger event discounts, spotlight advertising opportunities such as rotating features on the homepage, and invitations to exclusive networking events. They also gain access to extended training modules, early-bird access to new programs, and more visibility within the community.

Gold Membership

Gold Membership

At the top, the Gold membership delivers the most comprehensive benefits. Gold members enjoy maximum event discounts, premium advertising placements across newsletters and the homepage, and complimentary or VIP access to select flagship events. They also receive personalized consultation or training credits, recognition as top-tier supporters in major communications, and first access to pilot programs and leadership opportunities.
